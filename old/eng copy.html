XGIOVE, a band from the Marches italian region, was formed in 2018 following the meeting of Nicolò Buccioni, Giacomo Strappa, Andrea Massetti, Ludovico Bartolozzi and Federico Piermartire, who performed at numerous concerts and events under the name of ContrabbanD. They now feel the need to express themselves by writing original songs through which they can better express their own sense of identity. The sound is an intersection between the styles that characterize the individual components. The most evident styles are rock and folk, with a strong presence of electronic sounds and features of world music.


The sound is an intersection between the lines that characterize the individual components. The most evident themes are rock in various facets, folk, with the accordion as main instrument, and the contamination with various elements from the world of current music production.


Overcoming cultural and psychological barriers, the desire for redemption that contrasts the fear of living one's passions, going beyond a life designed by others; these are some of the elements present in their lyrics.


They participated with their "Il cuore sul cortile" at the "Festival dell'Adriatico" - Alex Baroni Award, and were awarded first place. On 7th June 2019 they were ranked 3rd on the podium of the Sanremo Rock & Trend national competition. During the last tour (2022) they took part in the line-up of various festivals, and were the opening acts at the concerts of Caparezza, Litfiba and Gabry Ponte.
